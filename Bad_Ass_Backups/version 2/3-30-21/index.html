Welcome to the Club!

The Badass Ladies Club is a community created to be in service to people who are committed to growing and healing via personal accountability, self reflection and of course laughter.

We don't claim to be experts, we are walking this messy path of personal growth and discovery right now. The journey is terrifying and rewarding, there is SO much to unpack around countless topics to learning who you are becoming.

As friends we have come to rely on one another for unconditional support and understanding when we hit hard feelings or revelations. This kind of friendship has been critical to our evolution, so we are turning it into a club, and You my Badass friend are invited to join!
